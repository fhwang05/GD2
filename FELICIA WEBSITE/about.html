MEMORY FOAM

Archive of the Lasting Effects on Earth left by Different Types of Pressure

ABOUT

Memory Foam is an archive of the lasting effects left on Earth by different types of pressure. From desire paths worn into grass by repeated footfall, to the polished grip of a door handle, to the unworn middle seat — this project documents the quiet impressions that accumulate over time.

Pressure takes many forms: the weight of a body, the flow of water, the persistence of habit, the avoidance of contact. Each leaves a trace. Memory Foam gathers these traces and asks what they reveal about how we move through, touch, and resist the world around us.
