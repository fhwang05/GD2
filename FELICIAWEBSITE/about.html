MEMORY FOAM

ARCHIVE OF THE LASTING EFFECTS ON EARTH LEFT BY DIFFERENT TYPES OF PRESSURE

WHAT IS THIS WEBSITE?


MEMORY FOAM is an archive of the lasting effects left on Earth. Categorized into 4 sections: Evolution, Evironmental, Repetitive Motion, and Architectural, as well as a section for lasting effects that I have found in my life. This website is a collection of images of these lasting effects, along with explanations of how they were formed and what they reveal about our world,or the impacts that they have on the world.

EVOLUTION:
The effects left by the process of evolution, showing how animals and plants have adapted to their environments over time, showing permanent changes in the natural world.
ENVIRONMENTAL:
The effects left by the environment, including natural disasters and human intervention (or lack of), showing the impact of events and actions, as well as the response of nature.
REPETITIVE MOTION:
Mainly categorized by desire lines, the effects left by continuous use or motion, showing human interaction and preference.
ARCHITECTURAL:
The effects left on buildings or by buildings, as well as decay and impact that is most easily observable in architecture, showing interaction and absence in the environment.

So what exaclty are lasting effects? They are the marks left behind by different types of pressure, whether it's the pressure of the environment, time, or human interaction. They can be both temporary and permanent, the important thing is that they show how one thing is affected by another. They are a display of both the passage of time and the effects and actions and change have on us and everything around us.
This website serves as a reminder of the effects we have on the world, and the effects that the world has on us.
